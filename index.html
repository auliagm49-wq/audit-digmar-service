Sample Case

Real work,
real results

Enterprise Energy Company — Oil & Gas Sector
Quarterly Digital Marketing Audit (2023–2025)
Challenge
  • Significant digital presence across website, paid ads, social media, KOL/BA collaborations, and content marketing — but no integrated performance visibility
  • Channel activities running in parallel without a unified performance framework or clear strategic alignment
Findings
  • Paid media generated majority of traffic, but traffic quality and funnel alignment were inconsistent
  • Organic visibility declined in high-intent industrial keywords, limiting inbound authority positioning
  • Website content structure did not fully support complex B2B buyer journeys
  • Social and KOL activities increased exposure but lacked measurable mid-funnel contribution
  • Competitors demonstrated stronger search positioning in technical topics
Outcome
  • Identified structural gaps across acquisition, engagement, and positioning layers
  • Mapped channel misalignment affecting digital ecosystem efficiency
  • Highlighted content and keyword opportunity clusters for authority growth
  • Delivered a prioritized 90-day restructuring roadmap across website, SEO, paid media, and content strategy
